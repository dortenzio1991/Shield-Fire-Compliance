Why It Matters

The inspector shouldn't be the one selling the fix.

Most fire compliance checks are run by the same company that installs and services the equipment. That's a conflict of interest baked into the report. We only inspect — so the result is the result, not a sales pitch.

01

No installs. No service contracts.

We don't sell extinguishers, alarms, or sprinkler work — so there's no incentive to find problems that aren't there.

02

A record, not an opinion.

Every finding is geo-tagged, timestamped, and photographed on site — an evidentiary trail your insurer and AHJ can rely on.

03

Filed the same day.

Reports go to your client portal and, where required, the local authority before the day is out — no weeks-long backlog.
