About

Built to remove the conflict of interest from fire compliance.

Shield Fire Compliance exists for one reason: the company that inspects your fire and life-safety systems shouldn't be the same one selling you the fix.

The Problem

Inspection and sales don't mix.

Across the industry, fire protection companies both inspect and service the same equipment. When the inspector's paycheck depends on finding — and fixing — problems, the report stops being neutral. Property owners, insurers, and AHJs are left trusting a document written by an interested party.

Our Answer

An inspector with nothing to sell.

We only inspect, document, and file. We hold no installation contracts, no service agreements, no equipment partnerships. Every report we produce is geo-tagged, timestamped, and filed the same day — a record built to be trusted precisely because we have no stake in what it says.

What We Stand For

Independence isn't a slogan — it's the whole model.

No conflict of interest

We never sell, install, or service fire protection equipment — full stop.

Evidence, not opinion

Geo-tagged, timestamped findings replace subjective judgment calls.

Filed the same day

A record that's out of date before it's filed isn't a record worth having.
