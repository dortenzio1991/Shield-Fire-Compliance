Services

Independent verification, standard by standard.

We check compliance — we don't sell, install, or service the equipment we're inspecting. Book a single check or a full annual cycle; every visit produces the same on-record report.

01

Fire Extinguisher Audit

Charge level, pressure, mounting, signage, and tag-date compliance verified against local code, checkpoint by checkpoint.

02

Alarm & Detection Check

Functional testing of smoke, heat, and pull-station devices, with panel and zone verification logged on site.

03

Sprinkler & Suppression Review

Visual and functional verification of wet, dry, and clean-agent suppression systems, including gauge and valve checks.

04

Egress & Exit Compliance

Exit signage illumination, emergency lighting, clear-path width, and door hardware audited against occupancy requirements.

05

Fire Door & Assembly Inspection

Fire-rated door and assembly checks — seals, closers, labels, and clearances — verified for rated-wall integrity.

06

Annual Compliance Certification

A scheduled, recurring inspection cycle that keeps every system's certification current and on file year-round.

07

Kitchen Suppression Check

Hood, duct, and ansul-style suppression system verification for commercial kitchens and food-service occupancies.

08

Multi-Site Portfolio Program

A single standardized inspection program run across every location in your portfolio, with one consolidated compliance dashboard.

The Line We Don't Cross

If something fails, we tell you exactly what and why — and hand you a record you can take to any licensed contractor of your choosing. That's the whole point of independent.
